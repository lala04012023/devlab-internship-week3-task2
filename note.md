# Key Findings

* The dataset contains **119,390 rows** and **32 columns**.
* **Cancellation rate:** City Hotel **41.7%**, Resort Hotel **27.8%**.
* **Highest booking demand:** **August** (13.8K bookings); **Lowest demand:** **January** (5.9K bookings).
* The **pivot table (Hotel × Arrival Month)** shows that **Resort Hotels outperform City Hotels during the summer season**.
* **Average lead time:** City Hotel **110 days**, Resort Hotel **92 days**.
* **Portugal (PRT)** has the highest number of bookings (**28.2K**).
* The **Transient** customer segment accounts for approximately **75% of all bookings**.
* Bookings with **longer lead times** tend to have **higher cancellation rates**.
